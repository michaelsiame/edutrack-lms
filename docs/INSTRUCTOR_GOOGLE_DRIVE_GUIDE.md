## ğŸ’¡ Common Questions

### **Q: Where are my files stored?**
A: When you select "Google Drive", files are automatically uploaded to a Google Drive folder. The link is stored in the database, and students download directly from Google Drive.

### **Q: Can students see my Google Drive?**
A: No! Students only get a public link to download the specific file. They can't access your Google Drive account or see other files.

### **Q: Can I delete files from Google Drive?**
A: Yes! When you delete a resource from the resource management page, it's also deleted from Google Drive automatically.

### **Q: What if upload fails?**
A: Common reasons:
- File too large (check size limits)
- Internet connection issue
- Invalid file type
- Google Drive quota full

Try again or contact your administrator.

### **Q: Can I switch between Local and Google Drive?**
A: Yes! Each file upload, you choose where to store it. Mix and match as needed.

### **Q: Are files backed up?**
A: Yes! Google Drive automatically backs up your files. You can also access them in your Google Drive dashboard.
